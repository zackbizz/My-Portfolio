My Projects

Employee Managment System

This system enables both employees and employers to have efficient and effective workdays. It offers two login options: one for employees and one for admins. Admins have the ability to monitor how many of their employees are on leave, how many employees have requested a leave, add new employees, manage employee leave, and even add new admins. For employees, the system allows them to apply for leave and provides real-time tracking of their leave status. Additionally, both admins and employees have access to the employee board, which enables them to view other employees' information and edit their own profiles, including their passwords. This system was done using html,css, javascript and PHP.

Student Managment System

Student Clearance System

This system has three logins: one for students, one for Staff(department head), and one for registrar(admin). Students can request clearance based on their student status. While the clearance is being processed, students can monitor the progress in real time. Each department head can either accept or decline the student's clearance request, depending on the student's status. Finally, the admin can accept or decline the clearance after reviewing the responses from each department head.

House Renting App

This app is a user-friendly house renting application that simplifies the process of finding and renting properties. With its intuitive interface and robust features, This app caters to both tenants and landlords. The app offers two distinct logins: one for 'Users' seeking rental properties and another for 'Landlords' looking to list their properties for rent. Users can easily browse through available properties and save their favorite listings. They can also communicate directly with landlords, schedule property visits, and complete the rental process seamlessly within the app. Landlords, on the other hand, can efficiently manage their property listings, review tenant applications, and communicate with prospective tenants. With a secure and transparent platform, This app aims to streamline the house renting experience for both tenants and landlords, fostering a hassle-free and efficient rental process. This app is done using React-Native and PHP.
